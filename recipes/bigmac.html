BigMac

Description

Two 100% beef patties, a slice of cheese, lettuce, onion and pickles. And the sauce.

Ingredients

  • Big Mac Bun
  • Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar Cheese Slice
  • Pickle Slices

Steps

  1. Fry beef patty
  2. Put patty on bun
  3. Put lettuce, cheese, and pickle on beef
  4. Squeeze sauce
